About Teyra Partners

A strategic advisory firm built for Central Asian markets

Teyra Partners delivers guidance across the full spectrum of M&A transactions, capital raises, and strategic growth initiatives. Our practice is grounded in deep regional knowledge — the people, the regulation, and the deal culture of Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, and Kyrgyzstan.

We provide internationally recognised, investor-ready structuring for every transaction we handle. We act as a genuine strategic partner, not just an intermediary.
